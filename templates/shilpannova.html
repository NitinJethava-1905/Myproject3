Shilpan NOVA

Elegance creation of SHILPAN NOVA is the joint venture of two experienced groups namely SHYAMAL & SHILPAN have been known signification for their best structural creativity in this city, working individually in this field since a long by developing numerous cementic structures.

Welcome to see and delight now we have planned to carve your beautiful world in the name of space, light and elevation with finest design at SHILPAN NOVA.

Experience landscape, experience nova.

Towers of SHILPAN NOVA are creatively crafted for fourteen flooring, consisting 3BHK with extra large space, fully ventilated, modernly facilitated, life with elegant foyer.

Above all finest elevation, lush green environment, 24X7 security system, car parking facility will be provided to make your life much more comfortable.

Experience the goodness of the creatively composed architectural design designed just to harmonize it with your dream of an ideal home.


Specifications
  • Vitrified tiles in all floor.
  • Anti-skid flooring in washyard.
  • Granite platform with S.S. or Quartz sink and designer wall tiles.
  • Provision for chimney and RO filter in kitchen.
  • Supreme branded quality sanitary wares & bath fittings.
  • Solar water supply in all bathrooms & provision for gas geyser in washyard.
  • Fire retardant copper wiring with modular switches & safety gear.
  • Aluminium powder coated window with child safety grill & provision for mosquito net.
  • Decorative main door and other plain flush doors with lock fittings.
  • White wall putty finish in internal walls.
  • Sand faced plaster with acrylic paint for external walls.
  • Intercom facility for each flat.
